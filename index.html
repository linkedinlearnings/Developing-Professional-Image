Understand the Culture

- Organizational Culture Shared Factors:
- Assumptions
- Beliefs
- Values
- Processes
- Code of Conduct
- Finding the right culture fit is key
- Research and ask questions about the Organization
- How do leaders communicate?
- What are the key messages?
- What kind of behaviour is awarded?
- What does the company stand for?
- What is company's mission?
- What are the unwritten rules?
- Develop your professional image to complenet your company's culture
- Part of research is aaccepting if the culture isn't right fit.

Avoid Cultural Mistakes

- Each organization has its own norms and systems already in place.
- How can you avoid mistakes?
- Research your new company
- Err on the side of caution
- Turn each challenge into a learning opportunity

Be a professional

- Part of being professional is how you come across to others
- Think about how you you can balance authenticity and professionalism
- Key areas:
- Phone Etiquette (Avoid making personal calls during business hours)
- Dress Code (Ask ahead of time, err on the side of caution)
- Timekeeping (Prepare to be at work early, and arrive to meetings early)
- Social Media (Minimize social media use during work hours)
- Communication (Consider your body language, consider email communications and norms)
